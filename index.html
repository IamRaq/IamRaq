Hello, world! Raq here!

Trained as an Engineer, I am an innovator who is passionate about projects that tackle problems of economic inequality and support community resilience. I am particularly interested in those at the intersection of Art, STEM and community development.

I currently work as a Program Coordinator at Brooklyn Workforce Innovations. At BWI I am currently improving the operational efficiency of the recruitment efforts of two of their trade program offerings. Outside of business hours, I am also a prominent local DJ and events curator --working on projects related to arts and culture and community building.

Previously, I received a graduate degree in Industrial Engineering from Northeastern University and have 5 years professional experience in the IT and IT Manufacturing sectors.

I spend most of my time in Boston, Mexico City and Brooklyn.
